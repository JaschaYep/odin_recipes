Veggie Burrito

Go back Home

Easy to make and very customizable, burritos are a perfect worknight dinner. Here's what you need and how you make them:

  • Cilantro-lime rice
  • Chipotle-spiced black bean
  • Taco-spiced fajita veggies
  • Zesty avocado cream sauce
  1. To a food processor, add avocado, cilantro, jalape√±o, garlic, lime juice, and sour cream (or Greek yogurt).
  2. Blend mixture stopping to scrap down the sides as needed, until mostly smooth. Set aside.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine dry rice with 2 cups of water and 1/2 tsp. salt.
  4. Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and gently simmer for 18 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in 3 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro, 1/2 tsp. lime zest, and 1 Tbsp. fresh lime juice.
  6. the rice cooks, heat 2 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Once hot, add bell peppers, poblano pepper, and red onion. Cook 7 to 9 minutes, stirring only occasionally, until tender. Stir in 1 Tbsp. taco seasoning and remove from heat.
  7. Drain and lightly rinse black beans. Shake off excess water and transfer to a bowl. Stir in adobo sauce, ground cumin, and a pinch of salt. Cover with a damp paper towel and microwave for 2 minutes, stopping at 1 minute to stir through, until warm.
  8. Assemble all parts and enjoy!
